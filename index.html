Our Services

Mangata and Gallo is a prestigious jewelry store specializing in occasions like engagements, weddings, and anniversaries. Operating primarily online with a storefront in Austin, Texas, their high-quality, classic designs have made the owner a well-known figure in the local jewelry scene.

About the Owner

Mariana, the owner and lead designer of Mangata and Gallo, graduated from design school specializing in diamond cutting and metal smithing. She opened a store in Austin and expanded her business online. Mariana's hands-on approach encompasses jewelry design, marketing, and e-commerce. Recently, she hired artisans to craft her designs and a young employee to manage the company's online presence. Together, they bring Mariana's captivating creations to life, captivating a growing audience with their exquisite beauty.
